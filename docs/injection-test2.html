What is a Language Model (LLM)?

A Language Model (LLM) is a type of artificial intelligence that is trained to understand, interpret, and generate human language. These models are trained on large datasets of text and learn to predict the likelihood of a sequence of words. This enables them to perform a variety of language-related tasks such as translation, summarization, question answering, and more.
